Galaga Derby

You player character is the Galaga Ship. Your objective in stage one is to clear all Asteriods. Golden Star's are Power UP's. It will add additional hit points to your ship. Careful though, firing upon the star will destroy it.

Game Controls

  1. ARROW keys are your primary controls.
  2. LEFT and RIGHT keys control Rotation.
  3. UP key controls Forward thrust.
  4. '.' or M key controls Missile Fire. NUMPAD 1 & 3 can be used too for those with a NUMPAD.
  5. RED Galaga ship similar to the Golden Star Power Up can be captured and used as additional FirePower. Careful though, firing upon the ship will destroy it. Optional configuration of how Ship 2 is played is in the Administration Panel.
  6. Warp Capability - Press ',', or DOWN key - Randomly disappear and reappear on another part of the screen. But watch out, you could warp into another object causing damage. Use at your own Risk. NUMPAD 2 can be used too for those with a NUMPAD.

Notes

  1. This game is best played on Full HD / 1080P resolution or higher. You can adjust the canvas display size and display scaling through the Administrative Game Settings.
