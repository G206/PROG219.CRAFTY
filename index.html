Galaga Derby

You player character is the Galaga Ship. Your objective in stage one is to clear all Asteriods. Golden Star's are Power UP's. It will add additional hit points to your ship. Careful though, firing upon the star will destroy it.

Game Controls

  1. ARROW keys are your primary controls.
  2. LEFT and RIGHT keys control Rotation.
  3. UP key controls Forward thrust.
  4. CTRL or SHIFT controls Missile Fire - DISABLE your Sticky Key Function. Registry Modification Instructions are available at this LINK. Z Key will also work as a backup fire key.

Notes

  1. This game is best played on Full HD / 1080P resolution or higher. You can adjust the canvas display size and display scaling through the Administrative Game Settings. It is recommended that you set the resolution as high as possible that will allow no scroll bars to be present.
